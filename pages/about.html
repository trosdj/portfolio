Graphic design works best when you look past the pixels and focus on telling stories.

I've been designing stories for a digital world since 2015.

Hello! I'm Dj Trost, a New York based graphic designer. I am, by heart, a storyteller and I use storytelling as a base for my design philosophy. My first graphic design teacher, a retired sign painter who taught a high school trade program, taught me that each design element should aid in telling that story.

In my free time, I play dungeons and dragons, play online roleplaying games, and I indulge in my love for horror films. As well, I have a deep respect for education, and would love to work with a brand that allows me to help aid in such a noble pursuit.

If you like my work, please reach out as I would love to get to know you. I cannot wait to learn about how I can help your brand tell it's story!
