StoreSMART

Social Media | Digital Marketing

StoreSMART is a Women-Owned small business that creates plastic organizational tools to help create a more orderly world. Although their main focus is business needs, they do offer quite a lot of direct to consumer products.

To help combat a misguided viral conversation around laminating your vaccine card, which in the end destroyed them, StoreSMART created the Vaccine Card Protector; a plastic sleeve that allows you to remove your card to add boosters or other information, while still protecting it from damage.

The campaign, developed and designed by me, was a huge success, generating over 1.8M impressions and significant revenue growth for the product. This campaign wouldn't have been as much of a success without the groundwork that I laid when I established a set of guidelines for marketing graphics, which were nonexistent when I started.

Alongside an infusion of stronger graphic design and keyword optimization, an emphasis was placed on how products can be used, and not just what they are. With such a wide breadth of products, it was important to break down the consumer groups that StoreSMART reached.

Business:
StoreSMART does a large amount of its business with companies, usually other small businesses, trying to organize themselves. It was clear that tips on adopting the best organization practices would be successful.

School:
StoreSMART's engagement with education stopped at folder sales. Bringing teachers and administrators to the whole catalog brought new opportunities for everyone.

Home:
While the marketing for StoreSMART was previously focused on B2B, a large part of their sales came from Amazon and Etsy. Bringing together tips for hobbies and home organization strengthened the relationship with an undeserved consumer base.
